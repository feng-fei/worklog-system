#### 2. H-F01: 表单验证规则错误——非维修单无法提交
- **文件**: [Create.vue:385-515](file:///c:/Users/Administrator/Documents/traework/frontend-web/src/views/records/Create.vue#L385-L515)
- **问题**: useFormValidation对所有工单类型都要求fault_description等维修字段必填，导致施工单/项目施工单无法提交
- **修复**: 将nextStep中的验证逻辑改为按工单类型动态验证：
  - **施工单**: 验证customer_name, work_address, title, work_content
  - **项目施工单**: 验证project_id, title, work_content（不需要customer_name）
  - **维修单**: 验证customer_name, work_address + fault_description/fault_diagnosis/repair_process
- **验证**: ✅ 三种工单类型均可成功创建

#### 3. H-B02: 创建工单时后端未保存expense_items
- **文件**: [records_routes.py:265-303](file:///c:/Users/Administrator/Documents/traework/backend/blueprints/records_routes.py#L265-L303), [records_routes.py:646-687](file:///c:/Users/Administrator/Documents/traework/backend/blueprints/records_routes.py#L646-L687)
- **问题**: 前端提交的expense_items参数，后端完全不处理，开支记录不会保存到财务支出表
- **修复**:

  - 正确设置category_id, amount, expense_date, remark, record_id, project_id
  - expense_type根据是否有project_id设为'project'/'daily'
  - update_record中先删除旧关联expenses再创建新记录
  - WorkRecord.to_dict()添加expense_items字段返回关联的开支列表（用于编辑回填）
- **验证**: ✅ 创建含2条开支的工单，详情页正确返回2条关联记录，合计金额正确

#### 4. H-B05: 物料库存日志缺少material_name和stock_before字段
